Mountain snails appear only during the rainy season and live in rocky crevices. Their meat is crunchy, sweet, and slightly fragrant with forest herbs. They're usually stir-fried with lemongrass and chili, steamed with ginger, or boiled.

ğŸŸ Grilled Fish with Straw
